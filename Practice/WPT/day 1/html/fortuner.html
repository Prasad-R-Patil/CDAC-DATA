Fortuner



The Toyota Fortuner is a mid-size SUV manufactured by the Japanese automaker Toyota since 2005. It is based on the Toyota Hilux pickup truck platform. The Fortuner is known for its ruggedness, versatility, and spacious interior.

It is powered by a range of diesel and gasoline engines, depending on the market. The Fortuner's body-on-frame construction and robust 4WD system make it suitable for both urban and off-road driving. The interior offers seating for up to seven passengers, making it a popular choice for families and adventurers.

The Fortuner is often equipped with features like a touchscreen infotainment system, climate control, and advanced safety features. It is a reliable and capable SUV designed to handle various terrains.
