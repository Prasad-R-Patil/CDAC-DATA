Dodge Black V6 Challenger



The Dodge Challenger is an American pony car manufactured by Dodge since 1970. The Challenger is one of the most iconic muscle cars in American automotive history. The black V6 model provides a blend of aggressive styling and balanced performance for daily driving.

The V6 Challenger is powered by a 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 engine, offering a good balance of power and fuel efficiency. It features a sleek, modern design while retaining the classic muscle car silhouette. Inside, you'll find a comfortable and well-equipped cabin with modern infotainment and safety features.

This model is popular for those who desire the Challenger's iconic looks without the higher fuel consumption of the V8 models. It's a great choice for cruising and making a statement.
